## Module Summaries

### Task 1 – Digital Literacy Infographic
I made a one-page colourful infographic in Canva explaining what digital literacy is, useful tools for students, safe internet practices, and professional online presence + email etiquette. Added it in the task-1-presentation folder.

### Task 2 – Student Digital Portfolio
Set up profiles on GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle. Created a GitHub README, filled my education on LinkedIn, and joined Kaggle. Screenshots are in the task-2-portfolio folder.

### Task 3 – Coding and Collaboration Platforms
Completed the “Solve Me First” beginner challenge on HackerRank and made a 5-question Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz using Google Forms. Screenshots and form link are in the task-3-platforms folder.

### Task 4 – Email Etiquette
Wrote two professional emails (one for assignment extension and one for internship application) and created a Social Media Do’s & Don’ts checklist. Both files are in the task-4-email-etiquette folder.

### Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness
Wrote a phishing case study on a college student and made a “Stay Safe Online” prevention checklist (with UPI tips and helpline 1930). Files are in the task-5-cybercrime folder.
